Privacy Policy for Proof Habit Tracker

Last Updated: January 4, 2025

Introduction

Proof Habit Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email Address: Required for account creation and authentication
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely (we cannot see your password)
  • User ID: Unique identifier assigned to your account

Profile Information

  • Name: First and last name (optional)
  • Username: Unique username for your profile
  • Bio: Optional biographical information
  • Profile Photo: Avatar image you upload

Content You Create

  • Habit Completions: Records of when you complete habits/tasks
  • Proof Photos: Images you upload as proof of habit completion
  • Captions: Optional text descriptions for your completions
  • Task/Habit Data: Habits you create, their descriptions, and settings

Social Features

  • Friend Connections: List of users you've connected with
  • Friend Requests: Incoming and outgoing friend requests
  • Challenge Participation: Your participation in challenges with friends
  • Likes and Comments: Interactions with other users' content

Device Information

  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens for sending push notifications
  • Device Type: iOS or Android platform information
  • App Usage Data: How you interact with the App

Permissions We Request

  • Camera: To take photos as proof of habit completion
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos as proof
  • Microphone: For video recordings (if applicable)
  • Notifications: To send you reminders and social notifications

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Provide Core Services
    • Create and manage your account
    • Track your habit completions and progress
    • Store and display your proof photos
    • Calculate and display your streaks
  2. Enable Social Features
    • Connect you with friends
    • Display your activity in the feed
    • Enable challenges and group activities
    • Facilitate likes, comments, and social interactions
  3. Send Notifications
    • Remind you about habit completions
    • Notify you of friend requests
    • Alert you to challenge updates
    • Send social notifications (likes, comments, etc.)
  4. Improve the App
    • Analyze usage patterns
    • Fix bugs and improve performance
    • Develop new features

Data Storage and Security

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase (supabase.com) to store and manage your data. Supabase provides:

  • Secure database storage (PostgreSQL)
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure file storage for photos
  • Authentication services

Your data is stored on Supabase's secure servers, which comply with industry-standard security practices.

Data Security

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  1. With Other Users: Your profile information, username, and public activity (completions, photos) are visible to users you've connected with as friends.
  2. Service Providers: We share data with Supabase, our hosting and database provider, to operate the App.
  3. Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  1. Access: You can view your data through the App's settings and profile screens.
  2. Update: You can update your profile information, bio, and avatar at any time in the App settings.
  3. Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us (see Contact Information below).
  4. Opt-Out of Notifications: You can disable push notifications in your device settings.
  5. Remove Friends: You can unfriend users at any time.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
